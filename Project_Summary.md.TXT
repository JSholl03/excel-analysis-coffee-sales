# Coffee Orders Sales Analysis (Excel)

## Project Snapshot

**Project Type:** Business & Sales Analysis (No-Code)

**Tools Used:** Microsoft Excel only (formulas, PivotTables, charts)

**Dataset:** Synthetic coffee sales data including orders, customers, and products

**Audience:** Hiring managers and business stakeholders

---

## Objective

To demonstrate strong **business analysis and Excel proficiency** by transforming raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights using **only native Excel functionality**.

This project focuses on how Excel can be used as a decision-support tool without relying on SQL, Python, or macros.

---

## What This Project Demonstrates

* Advanced use of **Excel formulas** to enrich and analyze data
* Ability to structure and relate multiple data tables
* Strong understanding of **sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability**
* Clear communication of insights through **dashboards and summary views**

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset simulates a coffee retail business and includes:

* **Orders table** – Line-level transaction data (sales, quantity, pricing)
* **Customers table** – Customer details and loyalty program participation
* **Products table** – Coffee type, roast, size, pricing, and profit

These tables are related through consistent IDs to enable analysis across customers, products, and regions.

---

## Key Excel Techniques Used

* **XLOOKUP / INDEX-MATCH** – Joining customer and product attributes to orders
* **SUMIFS & COUNTIFS** – Revenue, volume, and customer metrics
* **PivotTables & PivotCharts** – Aggregation by country, customer, product, and time
* **Calculated fields** – Sales and profitability metrics
* **Dashboards** – High-level KPIs and visual summaries

*No VBA, macros, SQL, or external tools were used.*

---

## Key Insights Generated

* Identified **top-performing customers** by total revenue
* Compared **sales performance by country** to understand geographic demand
* Analyzed **product mix and roast types** to evaluate profitability
* Assessed the impact of **loyalty card membership** on revenue contribution

These insights mirror common questions asked by sales, operations, and customer teams.
